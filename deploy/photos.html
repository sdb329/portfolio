familial ties

photography II, winter 2021

This collection of photos focuses on documenting my family and reflecting on certain items that create connections between others. During the pandemic, many families were not able to see each other. Even in hospitals, people were not allowed to see loved ones diagnosed with COVID because of how contagious it was. During this time, these objects provided memories that kept family together even when miles apart.
