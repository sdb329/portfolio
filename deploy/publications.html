intent magazine

visual communications IV, spring 2021

This is an original true crime magazine publication called “Intent.” This issue’s main story revisits a murder in the early 20th century that was originally deemed as a suicide. The article delves into why this was the court’s ruling despite the large evidence in place that pointed to the murder. This magazine features original photography as well as a photomontage of the original article that was published in 1920.
